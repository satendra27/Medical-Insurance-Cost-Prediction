About us - Mediacal cost insurance prediction

Welcome to our Medical Cost Insurance Prediction project! We are a team of data science and machine learning enthusiasts dedicated to leveraging the power of AI to solve real-world problems. Our project focuses on predicting individual medical insurance costs based on various personal and lifestyle factors such as age, BMI, smoking status, and region.

Project Goal:

Our aim is to help insurance companies, healthcare providers, and individuals better understand and anticipate medical expenses. By building a predictive model, we can offer insights into how different attributes affect insurance costs â€” enabling smarter pricing strategies and personalized planning.

What we did :

  • Collected and analyzed a medical cost dataset with demographic and health-related information.
  • Cleaned and preprocessed data for training.
  • Applied multiple machine learning models such as Linear Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost.
  • Selected the best-performing model based on accuracy and interpretability.
  • Deployed the model using a web interface for easy predictions.

Technolgies used

  • Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib)
  • Machine Learning algorithms
  • Flask for web interface
  • HTML?CSS (for frontend styling)

Our Vision

We believe predictive analytics in healthcare can play a crucial role in financial planning and preventative care. Through this project, we aim to demonstrate how data-driven decisions can lead to more transparency, fairness, and efficiency in the health insurance industry.
